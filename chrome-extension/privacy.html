QAtalyst Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how QAtalyst ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with your use of the QAtalyst Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

1. Information We Collect

The Extension processes the following data locally in your browser:

  • Jira ticket information (title, description, comments)
  • Configuration settings and preferences
  • API keys for third-party services (stored locally and encrypted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Generate test cases based on Jira ticket content
  • Maintain your extension preferences
  • Connect to third-party services (Confluence, Figma, Google Docs) when explicitly configured by you

3. Data Storage

All data is processed and stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not have servers that collect or store your data.

Your API keys are stored securely in Chrome's encrypted storage and are only used to make direct API calls to the respective services.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension integrates with the following third-party services when you provide the necessary credentials:

  • OpenAI/Anthropic/Google AI: For AI-powered test case generation
  • Confluence: To fetch linked documentation
  • Figma: To access design specifications
  • Google Docs: To retrieve linked documents

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services before connecting them to the Extension.

5. Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your preferences and settings
  • activeTab: To access the current Jira ticket content
  • Host permissions: To interact with Jira and other configured services

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • API keys are encrypted before storage
  • All data processing happens locally in your browser
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Withdraw API access at any time by removing the extension or revoking API keys

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@qatalyst.example.com.

By using the Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
